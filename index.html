Introduction

TinyWins ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

üîí Your Privacy Matters

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. TinyWins is designed with your privacy as the top priority.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information. TinyWins operates completely offline and does not transmit any data to external servers.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Any other personal or behavioral data

Data Storage

All data created in TinyWins (your habits, completion dates, streak information, and app preferences) is stored locally on your device only using your device's secure storage system.

This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Cannot be recovered if you lose your device or uninstall the app
  • Is not backed up to any cloud services by TinyWins

Permissions

TinyWins does not request any special permissions from your device. The app operates entirely within its own secure storage space without accessing your contacts, camera, location, or any other sensitive device features.

Third-Party Services

TinyWins does not integrate with, use, or share data with any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services
  • Any other external services or APIs

Children's Privacy

TinyWins does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. Since we don't collect any data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal information for us to access, modify, or delete. All your app data is stored locally on your device and is under your complete control. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling TinyWins from your device.
